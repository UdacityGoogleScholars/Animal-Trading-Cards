Marshall Pup

Marshall is a Dalmatian puppy!

  • Scientific Name: Dalmatian
  • Average Length: 23 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 13 years
  • Habitat: Dry open woodland

Marshall has spotted white fur covering his body and bright blue eyes. During missions, Marshall typically wears a firefighter helmet and a red vest. He also wears a red backpack, which features a water cannon (acting as a fire hose).
